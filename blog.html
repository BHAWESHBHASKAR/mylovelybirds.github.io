This Couple Took Their Vows By The Beach A Year After A Pandemic Court Marriage

Meet Pritisha and Amay

We are sure there are so many couples in the same boat as Pritisha and Amay! Couples who got married during lockdowns no thanks to the pandemic- but that was not how they wanted to get hitched! And now that the pandemic has put a pause on things for a while, they decided to go ahead and have the destination wedding that they wanted to have in the first place- a good year after their court wedding! We loved this cute celebration by the beach in Goa which looked like a hoot for everyone!

Amay and I swiped right on a dating app; however we didn’t really speak much via the app as I was doing my “background research”. He just moved back from the US where he lived for the last 11 years so I knew he was assessing his options. Once I knew he was a genuine guy and kind of cute, I casually started liking his Instagram pictures in the hopes of getting a response. A couple days later, he messaged me on Instagram to check what I was doing that night and we were both coincidentally at bars across the road from one another! He came to meet me immediately. Although we were with friends, we spent that entire night talking and felt an amazing energy between us that we’ve never felt with anyone else before. In fact, we took a picture together the first night we met, which I have hung up on the walls of our apartment. We started dating and he stood by me like a rock through the toughest of times (I lost my father in early 2020).
